Contest Information
Dates and Times
Submissions open July 14th, 2017
Submissions close August 20th, 2017
Public testing September 1st, 2017
Results announced September 8th, 2017
Scoring (Total scores are a percentage out of 100)
Public rating out of 5 (weighted 20%)
Gameplay out of 10 (weighted 40%)
Aesthetics out of 10 (weighted 25%)
Innovation out of 10 (weighted 15%)
Additional Bonus of 3% for XML with minimal errors
Details and Guidelines
Maps can be any size / team size
The only gamemode allowed is Destroy. This includes DTC/DTM. Mix maps with anything other than DTC/DTM will not be accepted
No more than 3 authors per map
Map must be a dry map and may not contain excess of TNT
You are allowed to be author on up to 3 maps, but only your top scored map will be counted (anyone over 3 maps will be DQ’d)
XML must be included when submitting a map
Maps can be submitted any time before the deadline
Maps must be packaged correctly for a submission to count
You are NOT allowed to ask a Map Developer for feedback on the map but may ask for XML assistance
Use [DMC] in the title of your submission to take part in the competition (ie: “[DMC] [DTC] Avalon Funland”)
Maps previously submitted to Stratus are not allowed to be entered into this competition
Maps must be submitted through Github
Prizes
First Strato, winner tag on discord
Second 1 month of Stratus-, runner up tag on discord
Third 1 stack of tokens
